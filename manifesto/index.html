  • Manifesto

I will always strive...

To keep the products that I build as simple as possible

To keep myself and my products aligned to their vision and true purpose

To build a company which allows its employees to work without unneeded stress

To build products that help improve peoples lives

To promote the creativity and ingenuity within others

My Dream

To build a selection of tools which people can use to help them live a richer and fuller life

To build a company which prides itself on working less and enjoying life more
