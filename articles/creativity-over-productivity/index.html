Creativity over productivity

All too often we give up early with side projects.

We are too focused on spinning them into products immediately and focusing on the building a company.

Not only does this add unneeded stress to ship fast but it also hampers quality of work.

Not everything needs to be a startup or a sass app straight out the door. Instead we should focus on being creative and taking the time to craft tools that we would utilise in our day to day lives.

The hypothesis here is that if you will be less stressed, more driven and happier. So your end result will be of a higher quality and have a greater chance of success.
