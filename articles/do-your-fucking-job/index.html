Do your fucking job

I had a recent bout of sadness regarding issues I faced in my workplace. The funny thing is that the issues didn't actually exist. It was all caused by me.

I would approach every situation with a feeling of negativity. My own lack of motivation would cause projects to fail and small details to pass through unnoticed. I was in a spiral of sadness because I lacked self-worth.

At the time I was fortunately reading a book called "The Messy Middle". It was written by the guy that founded Behance.net, which later sold to Adobe.

He had this chapter called "Do your fucking job", where he talked about how we need to do the hard things in order to progress, be unique and ultimately get shit done.

It was so profound to me that I now repeat the phrase, "Do you fucking job", before I go into any meeting or have to tackle a big problem.

I guess sometimes you just need a good old slap to the face to jump start things again.
