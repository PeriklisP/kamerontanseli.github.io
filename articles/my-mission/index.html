My Mission

To build products that help increase the daily productivity of 10,000 people.

What do I mean by productivity?

Sleep

We need at least 8 hours of sleep. Something not everyone currently has the habit of doing.

I will help more people sleep which will help them have more energy to tackle the tasks ahead of them.

Management

Essentialism is the key to ensuring people stay on top of work. Most todo lists help you list our tasks but non help you cut down on them.

I will help reduce the amount of tasks people focus on by teaching them the power of "no".

Movement

I talked about the great power of walking as a way to calm the mind and generate ideas. Exercise is important, it builds a framework around one's life and improves discipline and confidence in the individual.

I will help people move more throughout the day to ensure they stay healthy and have moments of calmness.

This will be my Ikigai
