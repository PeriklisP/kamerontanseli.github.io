Life is like a Jar

Getting Redder

The concept of a red team is designed to test a plan. What happens is, as you develop a plan -- you've got a problem and you develop a way to solve that problem -- you fall in love with it. You start to dismiss the short-comings of it, simple because, I think, that's the way the mind works...

Sometimes you're actually skipping over real challenges to it, or vulnerabilities in it, because you just want it to work. As we describe it, sometimes a plan can up being a string of miracles, and that's not really a solid plan.

Create a team of people who aren't wedded to the plan and ask them, "How would you disrupt this plan or how would you defeat this plan?". If you have a very thoughtful red team, you'll produce amazing results.
